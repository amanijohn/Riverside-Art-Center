Riverside
Art Center

Welcome to Riverside Art Center (RAC), a vibrant hub for creativity and connection in Wapakoneta! As a non-profit, we’re dedicated to inspiring and supporting local artists and art lovers of all ages. Through engaging classes, workshops, and exhibitions, RAC offers a space for learning, creating, and showcasing talent. We believe art is for everyone, and we welcome all, regardless of background or experience, to join our artistic community and explore the transformative power of art.
