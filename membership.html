Join Riverside

Welcome to Riverside Art Center (RAC), a vibrant hub for creativity and connection in Wapakoneta! As a non-profit, we’re dedicated to inspiring and supporting local artists and art lovers of all ages. Through engaging classes, workshops, and exhibitions, RAC offers a space for learning, creating, and showcasing talent. We believe art is for everyone, and we welcome all, regardless of background or experience, to join our artistic community and explore the transformative power of art.

Benefits

All members gain access to:

  • Free, unlimited admission to the art center and to special exhibitions.
  • Free programs, talks, and tours.
  • Member News emails with insider-only updates.
  • Access to priority Fast Pass line for our Tuesdays on the Terrace summer music series.
  • Up to 20% discount on events, programs, and performances.

More Ways to Support Us

If you have Community Market receipts, please bring them to Riverside Art Center, Community Market will support us with 2% of your non-acoholic purchases.  These funds support supplies to sustain our art center.
