About Us

Riverside Art Center (RAC) is a community-centered gallery and studio space that has been nurturing creativity in the heart of Riverside for over three decades. We are dedicated to providing a platform for local and regional artists to showcase their work, while fostering an inclusive and dynamic environment for the exploration of contemporary art. Through exhibitions, workshops, and public events, we aim to connect artists with the public, enriching the cultural fabric of our community. Whether you're an art enthusiast, emerging artist, or seasoned creator, Riverside Art Center welcomes you to discover, learn, and grow with us.
